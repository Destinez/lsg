Restaurants

    •  
Our team of experienced and dedicated professionals assists leading emerging and established restaurant brands and talent in the growth and protection of their businesses.

Overview

Many of the nation's leading emerging and established brands and much of the leading restaurant talent rely on Davis Wright Tremaine's national restaurant industry practice group to achieve their goals. Counting more than 60 professionals, in over a dozen key areas of law that intersect with the restaurant business, the DWT restaurant industry team guides clients through the ever-evolving restaurant industry.

Additional Areas of Focus

Employment & People
Employment & People

The restaurant business is one of the most people intensive of all industries. Clients rely on DWT to navigate all employment-related matters from incentive plans to dispute resolution.

IP and BP
Intellectual Property & Brand Protection

Intellectual property professionals at DWT work with restaurants around the globe to clear and protect trademarks and other valuable intellectual property assets.

RE & L
Real Estate & Leasing

DWT’s real estate and leasing team has specialized experience in helping restaurateurs navigate the real estate issues that matter most to restaurant industry clients.

Restaurant Finance and M&A

Restaurant Finance and M&A

Our restaurant team handles financings and transactions of all sizes, which include many of the industry's most prominent deals. From growth equity financings to purchases and sales of restaurant companies, we can guide you through the nuances of restaurant transactions.
