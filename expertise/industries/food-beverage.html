A Day in the Life

Here's just a sample of what we did on Thursday, January 22, 2022.

Agriculture

Advised global wine producer regarding agricultural workforce concerns.
Met with potential experts for regulatory agricultural proceeding.
Analyzed environmental conditions and water supplies to advise client in relation to the acquisition of agricultural land.
Revised purchase and sale agreement for large winery enterprise.
Conferred with potential expert witnesses for administrative hearing on agriculture pricing issues.

Alcohol

Drafted distribution agreement for artisan alcoholic beverage company.
Analyzed FAA Act and TTB regulations for a craft beer company.
Advised international wine company on retirement plan and fringe benefit plan issues associated with strategic transaction closing.
Developed strategy on behalf of family winery for purchase of vineyard property.
Reviewed trademark enforcement settlement proposal for craft brewery.
Researched multistate importation laws for wine suppliers.
Revised promissory note and security agreement for purchase of winery equipment.
Prepared strategy for development of celebrity-endorsed beverage brand.
Evaluated investment documents on behalf of non-alcoholic beverage producer.
Provided wine producer with trademark registration and infringement risk strategy.

Food + Beverage

Corresponded with plant-based food producer regarding investor stock redemption right.
Discussed credit facility proposal with organic baby food company.
Conferred with food producer concerning status of FDA Consent Decree.
Discussed organization and advisor agreements with confectionary company.
Drafted and negotiated merger agreement for large food company.
Revised recipe development agreement for natural supplement client.
Drafted warehouse and fulfillment agreement for seafood canning company starting to provide 3PL services for its customers.
Negotiated supply agreement for chocolate company with its major distributor.
Led strategy discussion regarding possible infringement claim for coffee producer.
Counseled seafood distribution company concerning transaction agreement documents.
Updated employee handbook for family-owned catering company.
