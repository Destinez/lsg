Overview

Our integrated team of financial services, technology, privacy, and corporate lawyers—recognized as leaders in the nation by Chambers Fintech—takes a collaborative approach to helping financial institutions, emerging FinTechs, and other companies launch innovative products or invest in new solutions.

We work with every part of the FinTech ecosystem and can help clear the hurdles—regulatory, organizational, and cultural—companies face when launching new products. We can help you identify potential pitfalls and emerging trends as you put your plan together. And, we scale our team and level of involvement to wherever you are in your development life cycle. By collaborating with clients and applying people, process, and technology in new ways, we deliver efficient service that focuses on helping you meet both legal and strategic goals. We also design fee arrangements that focus on cost certainty and value delivered.

Client Activities in FinTech

We’ve assisted clients with a wide range of FinTech products and services, including, but not limited to:
Alternative and marketplace lending
Bill-payment solutions
Crowd sourcing/funding
Cryptocurrencies, authentication systems and platforms
Digital wallets of all kinds
Innovations in retail banking (such as internet banking)
Mobile, in-app, e-commerce or POS payment solutions
Online financial platforms
P2P money transfers and remittances
Trading platforms
Transaction monitoring and screening systems
