We navigate clients through the maze of federal and state laws regulating the residential mortgage lending and servicing industry.

Overview

As the mortgage industry and regulatory landscape continues to change, we work to minimize our clients' risk in line with their commercial objectives and consumer focus.

Our team combines regulatory depth, fierce and agile skill in litigation all the way to the appellate level, and a strategic and sensitive approach to government investigations and enforcement actions. Our bench includes highly-specialized practitioners and former regulators – including those who served at the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, offices of state attorneys general, U.S. Attorneys' offices, the DOJ, the Federal Reserve, and the U.S. Treasury Department – whose experience informs our approach.

We use our firsthand knowledge to aid bank and non-bank mortgage lenders, loan servicers, secondary market investors and FinTechs in all aspects of mortgage origination and servicing-related enforcement, supervision, regulatory and compliance counseling, and complex litigation. This includes areas such as fair lending and fair and responsible servicing issues, disclosure and timing requirements, privacy and information security, marketing and advertising issues, lender-placed insurance, loan modifications and other loss mitigation programs and issues, foreclosure and bankruptcy.

Industry Knowledge

Our intimate knowledge of laws and regulations governing the residential mortgage industry includes:

  • The Truth in Lending Act (TILA)
  • The Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA)
  • The Home Ownership and Equity Protection Act (HOEPA)
  • The Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)
  • The Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA)
  • The Fair Housing Act (FHA)
  • The Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA)
  • The Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA)
  • The Secure and Fair Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing (SAFE) Act
  • The Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (SCRA)
  • Privacy laws such as the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act
  • Issues arising under the False Claims Act (FCA)
  • Issues arising under unfair, deceptive and abusive acts and practices laws

And their state law equivalents.

Litigation

We litigate mortgage lending and servicing disputes arising under all of these laws and more. We defend clients nationwide in class actions, single-plaintiff claims, regulatory proceedings, and other complex mortgage litigation, and our consumer financial services litigators include skilled trial attorneys and appellate practitioners.

Loss-mitigation related claims*

Wrongful foreclosure and other mortgage-related appeals*
