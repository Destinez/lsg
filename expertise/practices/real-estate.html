Real Estate

    •  
Real estate runs on relationships, and we have them.

Overview

From coast to coast, we remove roadblocks to complex transactions. Developers, investors, financial institutions, management companies, large tenants, and brokers rely on us to get the full range of deals done nationwide quickly and cost-effectively. We have particular experience in public-private partnerships, siting essential public facilities, and development from the ground up. And we combine a full-service team with a commitment to relationships to ensure our lawyers create opportunities, not obstacles.

Relationships = Results
From our long experience as a leading firm in real estate, we know the business and political leaders in our communities and are respected by opposing counsel whose cooperation and trust help complete projects. We are zealous advocates, but with a respectful approach that smooths the path for you and preserves your reputation in the community.

We put the same effort into our relationship with you. Many of our clients stay with us for decades and that close engagement helps us understand your business context and complete your deals efficiently. So you get what you need from us, when you need it, at a predictable cost. Our commitment to relationships is probably why most of our clients come through referrals, including from those on the other side of past transactions. We even serve as real estate "matchmakers" by introducing clients who then join forces on large projects.

One-stop shop = Easy
We support our deep real estate, land use, and construction teams with highly regarded practitioners focused on environmental issues, tax, and finance. We work together, not in silos, so you can rest easy knowing our advice is coordinated, not fractured. We are unfazed by unusual challenges and not intimidated by scale or speed. And we save you the effort, expense, and administrative hassle of assembling and managing multiple legal providers.

Charting the Future
Our clients rely on us to navigate the real estate sector, but also look to us for advice on how to help lead the change. We want to help you chart your future and lead the change. (View DWT's climate and sustainability commitment.)

Niche Services

Public-Private Partnerships

A collaborative team of attorneys with strong community and government ties is crucial to getting public-private projects off the ground. We have introduced driven developer clients to public landowners to kick-start stalled hotel projects, negotiated a variety of ground leases between public and private entities, and helped to move along massive infrastructure projects. Whether we are negotiating financing and ownership structures or ground leases, our understanding of each party’s core objectives drives our ability to find common ground. SeaTac Airport’s North Satellite Terminal, “The Clement” Intercontinental Hotel Monterey, AC Hotel by Marriott at the Port of Vancouver, King County’s regional library services facility, and several transit-oriented developments in Puget Sound and San Jose stand as just some of the testaments to our team’s aptitude in this area.

Essential Public Facilities

Our knowledge of Washington’s unique essential public facilities landscape and experience working in cities and jurisdictions throughout the State make us ideally-suited to handle land use permitting and, if necessary, dispute resolution for facilities facing local opposition. We have represented clients in the siting of psychiatric, correctional, and public transit facilities across the state.

Multi-site Acquisitions

Few firms have both the experience and deep, cross-practice teams necessary to complete nationwide multi-site acquisitions quickly and efficiently. Franchisees and other rapidly expanding entities benefit from our knowledge-sharing culture because we can provide practical advice rooted in prior successful transactions. Our real estate attorneys collaborate with a diverse and experienced team of corporate, tax, and environmental attorneys to efficiently complete deals involving dozens of acquisitions from coast to coast. In recent years, our team has represented numerous restaurant and retail franchisees in the acquisition of franchised restaurants, including recent portfolio acquisitions of 125 Pizza Huts and 365 Arby’s restaurants. Not intimidated by deal size or complexity, we pride ourselves on guiding our clients every step of the way from letter of intent to closing.

Industry Focus

Airports

Airports have more regulatory considerations than perhaps any other property type. From rural airports to developing regional airfields and major international airports, our real estate and land use team advises clients on a variety of projects. Recently, we developed and implemented a public-private partnership for the redevelopment of the North Satellite Terminal at SeaTac airport and are supporting entities including Alaska Airlines in the development of the new passenger terminal at Paine Field in Everett, Washington.

Healthcare

In the rapidly changing industry, healthcare entities need the flexibility to transform their real estate to meet their needs. Our real estate lawyers work closely with our nationally prominent healthcare practice to serve clients on complex projects requiring a multi-disciplinary and collaborative approach. This includes strategic growth initiatives for our clients, such as a joint venture between Providence and Walgreens to operate express care centers in Washington and Oregon stores.

Hotels & Resorts

We provide business-focused legal advice to owners, operators, developers, and investors. We have worked on projects nationwide, from Alaska’s largest ski resort to a 5-star mountain resort in Colorado.

Public Transit

With increasing density coming to urban population centers, public transit agencies are actively expanding their systems to meet demand. Our lawyers have the deep experience in applicable state and local laws and Federal Transit Administration rules and regulations as well as the local relationships necessary to limit unnecessary and costly delays. Our robust experience includes real estate acquisition, entitlement, and construction for development of new public transit facilities; disposition and development agreements, real estate sales and ground leases for Transit Oriented Developments; public-private partnerships for joint use and development of transit centers; acquisition of railroad use easements and railroad right of way; and land use proceedings when siting opposition arises. We represent both public transit agencies and private property owners, developers, and contractors.

Restaurants

From individual leases to multi-site market acquisitions, our team supports restaurants nationwide. We are currently representing Punch Bowl Social in their national lease rollout and work with restaurants like MILA Miami on their initial lease.

Seafood Processing

With close ties to the industry, we represent a variety of seafood clients in real estate, land use, and corporate matters, including Cooke’s acquisition of Icicle Seafoods - one of the largest transactions in the industry.

Timber

The U.S. timber industry has a storied history and promising future, even as it addresses sustainability, climate change, and other challenges. We proudly represent owners of Northwest timberlands in acquisitions, dispositions, other transactions, and development.

Telecommunications

With decades of experience working with and in the communications industry, our attorneys have developed strong relationships and reputations on a national level. We assist wireless, cable, fiber optic, neutral host and landline companies, with everything from entitlement and acquisition of support structures to leasing of office space. Additionally, our land use attorneys boast experience with zoning issues, rights-of-way access permits, and public hearings before a variety of agencies and governmental bodies.

Construction

Need a design/build contract reviewed or drafted? Our team helps owners, general contractors, and design professionals build successful projects.
