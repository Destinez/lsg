The Dotted Line Podcast by DWT

Technology is meant to enrich our lives and help us overcome complex problems, but getting your clients to integrate with it from a legal standpoint can be anything but a one-touch business solution. Fortunately, our seasoned technology transactions attorneys have seen just about all of it. So whether you're just getting your feet wet in the exciting world of commercial contracts or are in-house counsel looking to sharpen your skills, this podcast has something to offer you.

We use our industry knowledge to help companies across the tech space get deals done quickly and efficiently, navigate the federal, state, and local regulatory landscape, and effectively litigate all forms of cases.

Overview

For decades, Davis Wright Tremaine has served small, medium, and large tech companies in Silicon Valley, Seattle, and beyond. We have built one of the nation's leading teams covering the full range of transactional, counseling, litigation, and intellectual property issues involving technology, privacy, and data security. We work by your side to bring your products and services to market faster and better and grow your existing businesses, with less risk of regulatory interference, and to solve the full scope of legal problems that may affect your bottom line.
