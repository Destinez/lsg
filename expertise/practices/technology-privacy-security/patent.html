Patent

    •  
Innovation is how your business stands apart in a competitive market. We protect those innovations - and help you maximize their value.

Overview

Like many good patent firms, we have deep technical expertise. But, obtaining patents for our clients isn’t our sole focus. We know it’s not enough to get patents for the sake of getting patents, but that the focus must be on obtaining the most valuable ones, the ones that provide a competitive advantage. We combine legal, technical, and business insight to help you build global portfolios that set your business up for success, now and in the future.

How we can help
We work with you to obtain patents and integrate them into your business strategy. We develop patent licensing strategies—either separate from or as part of a patent enforcement strategy—and provide due diligence services related to M&A activity. We assist with post-grant proceedings such as reexaminations, oppositions and post-grant review. We also assist with resolution of patent disputes and, when necessary, litigation.

We provide assistance to research institutions and other entities with technology transfer operations – whether as a supplemental legal resource, or as a nearly comprehensive outsourced technology transfer office.

Technological and industry insight
Our team includes 25 attorneys and patent agents with bachelor’s, master’s and/or doctoral degrees in a variety of fields including biotechnology, computer science/engineering, electrical engineering, microelectronics, and control systems. We combine that technical expertise with industry-leading practices in software, media/entertainment, communications, financial services, food and beverage, and healthcare to provide business and strategic insight in addition to legal counsel.

We have particular depth of experience in:

  • software and hardware
  • cloud computing
  • artificial intelligence
  • data security
  • payments, e-commerce, and FinTech
  • mobile communications
  • blockchain
  • life sciences and agriculture
  • autonomous vehicles
  • medical devices
  • mechanical engineering

Our Approach

Understand your challenges, focus on your goals
Many of our team members worked as engineers, programmers, developers, and project managers before becoming lawyers, and many have worked in-house. We have firsthand insight into the business and operational challenges you face, and our experience informs our approach. We take a holistic view of your intellectual property portfolio and design solutions specifically for your business. For emerging companies, that might mean something different than it would mean for a multi-national corporation. Each of our engagements includes a continuous focus on your business and strategic goals.
