Careers

Explore a world of limitless opportunities and watch your career soar

Exceptional legal talent propels us forward, and we are always on the lookout for talented professionals to join our team. Since we are a full-service law firm offering transactional, advisory, and dispute resolution services, we are confident that there is an exciting opportunity for everyone, no matter your area of expertise.

While we are extremely focused on providing high-quality advice to our clients, we also strive hard to create an environment where you can thrive as a legal professional and enjoy a rewarding career.

Whether you are a fresh graduate or an experienced lawyer, exciting opportunities await you at Templars Law LLC. To apply, send in your CV to mail@templarslaw.sg

Why should you work with us?

At Templars Law LLC, every lawyer gets an equal shot at building an exciting career by carving a path for themselves. For us, you are not just a lawyer — you are a potential changemaker who holds power to transform the legal profession. We firmly believe that our biggest asset is the people we work with. We foster a collaborative working environment that values communication, collaboration, and teamwork above anything else.

Learn as you grow when you work alongside warm, friendly and experienced colleagues.

Join as a trainee

We offer several Practice Training Contracts each year and invite bright and talented individuals to apply. We offer our trainees exposure to a gamut of matters and various aspects of Singapore law.

Our varied training program and the mentorship by a supervising partner ensure that you receive comprehensive training in matters related to specific practice areas and develop the necessary soft skills as a lawyer.

We are looking for talented yet personable law graduates who can contribute positively to our firm. If this sounds like you, we look forward to hearing from you!

Join as an intern

Eager to get an ‘on the job’ experience before you get formally inducted into the world of law?

Get a glimpse of what goes on in a law firm by applying to our internship program. You will be able to better understand the type of work each practice area entails and get a first-hand experience of watching lawyers in action. To make your internship more meaningful, you will be assigned a mentor during your time with us.

As an intern, you will get ample opportunities to hone your research, drafting, analytical skills and also develop your interpersonal skills. You will also get a chance to attend court hearings, client meetings and simply experience first hand what life is like working at a law firm!

Write in to us to apply for an internship experience!
